This is my favorite sushi place to go to! The sushi rolls here are so good and they're not that pricey. This restaurant is a small place that not many people know of, but I would really recommend anyone to go here! It's family owned so only a husband and a wife are running the restaurant. However the service is not slow at all! My favorite rolls are the Mango Gogo and the Salmon lovers. I come here about every 2-3 weeks but I never get tired of it!.

Love this place! It's tucked away and quiet. Tony and his wife are always friendly and give us great service. If you are wanting a big sushi place with quick in and out service, this is not really that place. We expect to sit down, chat, have some drinks, and eat leisurely. We love the rolls and cold drinks. I would recommend this place for a mom and pop feel and consistently good sushi. The salmon skin hand rolls are the best in town!
